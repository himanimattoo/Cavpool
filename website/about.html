About Cavpool

Building safer campus transportation, one ride at a time.

Our Mission

Cavpool was created to address the transportation challenges faced by university students. We believe that getting around campus and the surrounding area should be safe, affordable, and reliable. Our platform connects students with verified drivers from their own university community, creating a trusted network of transportation options.

🛡️ Safety First

Every driver undergoes verification and background checks. Real-time tracking and emergency contacts ensure peace of mind for every ride.

🎓 Student-Focused

Built by students, for students. We understand the unique transportation needs of campus life and student budgets.

🤝 Community-Driven

Connecting members of the same university community creates trust and accountability that traditional rideshare apps can't match.

Our Story

Cavpool began as a capstone project at the University of Virginia, born from our team's firsthand experience with campus transportation challenges. As students, we recognized the need for a safer, more reliable way to get around campus and to nearby locations.

Traditional rideshare services often left students waiting long periods, especially during peak times or late nights. More concerning was the lack of accountability and safety features specifically designed for the campus environment. We knew there had to be a better way.

Our solution focuses on creating a trusted community where students can rely on verified drivers from their own university. By requiring university affiliation and implementing comprehensive safety features, we've built a platform that parents trust and students prefer.
