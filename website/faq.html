Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Cavpool's campus transportation service.

Getting Started

What is Cavpool? ▼

Cavpool is a specialized ridesharing platform designed specifically for university students and campus communities. We connect riders with verified drivers from the same university, creating a safer and more trusted transportation network than traditional rideshare services.

Who can use Cavpool? ▼

Cavpool is available to verified university students, faculty, and staff. Currently, we're operating at the University of Virginia, with plans to expand to other universities. All users must be at least 18 years old and provide valid university credentials for verification.

How do I sign up? ▼

Download the Cavpool app (coming soon), create an account using your university email, and complete the verification process. You'll need to provide your student/faculty ID and may need to complete additional verification steps depending on whether you want to be a rider, driver, or both.

Is Cavpool free to use? ▼

Cavpool is free to download and sign up. Riders pay for individual rides, with pricing designed to be affordable for students. Drivers earn money by providing rides. We charge a small service fee to maintain the platform and safety features.

Safety & Security

How does Cavpool ensure rider safety? ▼

Safety is our top priority. We require university verification for all users, conduct background checks on drivers, provide real-time GPS tracking, allow emergency contact notifications, and maintain 24/7 support. All rides are tracked and users can rate their experience.

What verification is required for drivers? ▼

Drivers must provide university verification, valid driver's license, current vehicle registration, proof of insurance, and pass a background check. Vehicle inspections may also be required. We continuously monitor driver ratings and feedback.

Can I add emergency contacts? ▼

Yes! You can add multiple emergency contacts who can be automatically notified when you're on a ride. They'll receive your ride details and can track your location in real-time for added peace of mind.

What should I do if I feel unsafe during a ride? ▼

Use the in-app emergency button to immediately contact your emergency contacts and our support team. You can also call 911 if necessary. All rides are GPS tracked, so we can locate you quickly. Report any safety concerns immediately through the app or by emailing security@cavpool.app.

Using the Service

How do I request a ride? ▼

Open the Cavpool app, enter your pickup and destination locations, choose your preferred ride time, and request a ride. You'll be matched with a verified driver from your university community. You can see driver details and estimated arrival time.

Can I schedule rides in advance? ▼

Yes! You can schedule rides up to 24 hours in advance. This is especially useful for airport trips, early morning classes, or planned events. Advanced scheduling helps ensure driver availability.

What if I need to cancel my ride? ▼

You can cancel rides through the app. Cancellation policies vary based on timing - free cancellation is usually available until a few minutes before pickup. Late cancellations may incur a small fee to compensate drivers for their time.

Can I share rides with friends? ▼

Absolutely! You can add friends to your ride when booking. This helps split costs and is more environmentally friendly. All passengers must be verified Cavpool users.

Payments & Pricing

How much do rides cost? ▼

Pricing is designed to be student-friendly and competitive with other transportation options. Costs vary based on distance, time of day, and demand. You'll see the exact price before confirming your ride. We aim to keep prices affordable for student budgets.

What payment methods are accepted? ▼

We accept all major credit and debit cards through our secure payment processor (Stripe). Digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay are also supported. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

Do I need to tip drivers? ▼

Tipping is optional but appreciated. You can add a tip through the app after your ride. Our pricing is designed to fairly compensate drivers, but tips are a great way to show appreciation for excellent service.

For Drivers

How do I become a Cavpool driver? ▼

Apply through the Cavpool app by providing your university verification, driver's license, vehicle information, insurance proof, and complete a background check. You'll also need to pass our driver training program and vehicle inspection.

How much can I earn as a driver? ▼

Earnings vary based on how often you drive, time of day, and distance of trips. Drivers keep the majority of each fare, with Cavpool taking a small service fee. Peak times (evenings, weekends) typically offer higher earning potential.

What are the vehicle requirements? ▼

Vehicles must be in good condition, pass safety inspection, have current registration and insurance, and accommodate at least 3 passengers. Age requirements vary by location but generally vehicles should be less than 15 years old.

Technical Support

What if the app isn't working properly? ▼

Try restarting the app or your device first. If problems persist, contact our support team at support@cavpool.app or through the in-app support feature. Include details about your device and the specific issue you're experiencing.

Is Cavpool available on both iOS and Android? ▼

Yes! Cavpool will be available on both iOS and Android devices. We're currently in development and will announce availability dates soon. Follow our updates for the latest information.

How do I update my account information? ▼

Access your profile through the app settings to update personal information, payment methods, emergency contacts, and preferences. Some changes (like university affiliation) may require additional verification.
